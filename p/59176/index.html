Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pushup Bank (‚Äúwe,‚Äù ‚Äúour,‚Äù or ‚Äúus‚Äù). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Pushup Bank handles your information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share your personal information.

Pushup Bank is designed to work without accounts and without our own backend server. We do not track your usage, location, or identity, and we do not run analytics or advertising SDKs.

3. Data Storage

Pushup Bank stores your data in the following places:

  1. Locally on your device: Your push-up sessions and settings are stored in the app‚Äôs sandbox.
  2. iCloud (optional): If you enable iCloud Sync in the app, your data is synced using your private iCloud database (CloudKit) under the iCloud container iCloud.pushup.bank.data.

We (the developers) cannot see or access your private iCloud database from the CloudKit Dashboard.

Examples of data stored by Pushup Bank may include:

  • Push-up session records (date/time and count)
  • App settings (e.g., reminder schedule, sync toggles)
  • Profile customization you set in the app (e.g., nickname, avatar)

4. Third-Party Services

Pushup Bank may use Apple system services you choose to enable:

  • iCloud / CloudKit (optional): Used to sync your data across your devices.
  • Apple Health / HealthKit (optional): Used to write your push-up counts to the Health app.
  • Notifications (optional): Used to schedule local reminders on your device.

We do not use third-party analytics services and we do not display ads.

5. Optional Features

Apple Health / HealthKit (Optional)

If you enable Health Sync, Pushup Bank will request permission to write push-up count samples to Apple Health.

  • Pushup Bank does not read your Health data.
  • You can revoke permission at any time in iOS Settings (Health app ‚Üí your profile ‚Üí Apps) or by turning Health Sync off in the app.
  • The app also provides an option to delete push-up samples that were written by Pushup Bank.

Reminders (Optional)

If you enable reminders, Pushup Bank schedules local notifications on your device. Reminder scheduling does not require an account, and no reminder data is sent to our servers.

Data Export / Import

You can export your session history to a JSON file and import it back later. Exported files are saved wherever you choose (e.g., Files app, iCloud Drive) and are not uploaded to our servers.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect your personal data on our servers, there is no need to request data deletion from us.

To delete your data:

  • Delete the app to remove local data from your device.
  • If you enabled iCloud Sync, you can also manage and delete app data through your iCloud storage settings.
  • If you enabled Health Sync, you can remove Pushup Bank‚Äôs Health permissions and delete push-up samples (either from the app‚Äôs delete option or within the Health app).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by updating the date at the top of this policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the App Store support page.
